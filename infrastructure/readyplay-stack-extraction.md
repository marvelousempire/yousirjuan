# ReadyPlay Infrastructure Extraction

This document maps the production-proven infrastructure concepts extracted from the ReadyPlay ecosystem and adapted into You-Sir Juan.

The goal is to preserve:

- production reliability

while replacing sports-specific functionality with sovereign AI infrastructure.
