# Hardware Engineer Agent - Wish List

**Last Updated:** May 27, 2026

This agent serves as the single source of truth for **all physical hardware** across the family office, business, and studio.

| Priority | Capability | Description |
|----------|------------|-----------|
| 1 | Complete Hardware Inventory | Track every computer, laptop, iMac, server, and workstation with full specs, location, and status |
| 2 | Networking Equipment | Track all routers, switches, access points, modems — including port counts, speeds, and firmware |
| 3 | Living Hardware Ledger | Maintain purchase date, cost, warranty status, depreciation, and support contracts |
| 4 | Port & Connectivity Knowledge | Know exact port configuration on every device (USB-C count, Thunderbolt, HDMI, Ethernet, USB-A, etc.) |
| 5 | Storage & Disk Tracking | Track all internal SSDs, HDDs, external drives, and NAS systems with health and backup status |
| 6 | Shopping Lists | Maintain live Amazon and eBay wishlists with current prices and direct links |
| 7 | Cable & Peripheral Management | Track every cable, wire, adapter, dongle, and peripheral with quantity and location |
| 8 | Physical Location Tracking | Know the exact physical location of every item (room, rack, desk, studio, office, home, storage) |
| 9 | Hardware Reporting | Generate on-demand reports and audits by location, by type, by age, or by cost |
| 10 | Upgrade Recommendations | Analyze current hardware and recommend upgrades/replacements with cost estimates |
| 11 | Compatibility Knowledge | Understand what devices and cables work together |
| 12 | Audit & Reconciliation | Support physical audits and keep the digital ledger perfectly reconciled |

**Mission:** This agent must know more about our physical technology assets than any human in the organization.
