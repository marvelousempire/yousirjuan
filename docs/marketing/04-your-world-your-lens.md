# Your World, Your Lens

**Tagline:** Same household. Same data. Completely different worlds.

---

## What it is

Every family member who authenticates into You-Sir Juan™ sees a different interface — tailored not just in name, but in color, layout, vocabulary, typography, mood, and visual depth. The function is identical for everyone. The form belongs to each person.

---

## Why it matters

A family office principal thinks differently than their partner. A technically-minded household member reads information differently than someone who wants warmth and simplicity. A founder sees their home operations through a different lens than a family member who just wants to check the schedule.

Most interfaces force everyone into the same view. You-Sir Juan™ does the opposite: it reads the member and renders their world.

---

## The five paradigms

| Paradigm | Visual mood | Label language | Layout | Who it fits |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| **Executive** | Obsidian — deep purple `#7C5CFF` | Today · Briefings · Operations | Dense 3-column grid | Principals, executives, decision-makers |
| **Warm** | Copper — amber `#FF6B35` | Your day · On your list · Home | Soft vertical stack | Partners, family members who want warmth |
| **Technical** | Matrix — neon green `#00FF88` | Session · Queue · Runtime | Developer-dense grid | Builders, operators, technical members |
| **Sovereign** | Gold — `#FFD700` | Agenda · Directives · Domain | Command center | Founders, household heads |
| **Casual** | Adaptable | Right now · Stuff to do · World | Open cards | Younger members, lighter users |

---

## How it works

1. At authentication, the backend returns the member's full paradigm definition — palette, layout, label set, typography, mood
2. The interface applies CSS custom properties (`--bg`, `--fg`, `--accent`) and switches layout class instantly
3. The Associate Agent's greeting reflects the member's paradigm tone
4. If the member edits their paradigm via the Settings screen, the change propagates live to all connected devices via SSE

---

## Who it's for

Any household with more than one person. The moment a second family member signs in and their world looks and feels different from the first member's world — that's the product working exactly as designed.
