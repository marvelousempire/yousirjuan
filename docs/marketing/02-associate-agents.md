# Associate Agents

**Tagline:** Your own AI — shaped by you, named by you, speaking in a voice you chose.

---

## What it is

Every family member gets a personal Associate Agent — a persistent AI presence that speaks for the platform, remembers everything about their relationship with it, and acts as the intelligent intermediary between them and every system the household runs on.

The Associate is not a chatbot. It is not a generic assistant. It is a named, voiced, character-consistent AI companion that the member shapes from the moment they onboard.

## Why it matters

The AI you interact with should feel like yours. Not like a product everyone else uses. The Associate Agent is the personality layer that makes You-Sir Juan™ feel like a member of the household rather than a piece of software.

Every Associate has:
- A name the member knows it by
- A voice the member selected at setup, running locally on the platform
- A system prompt tuned to that member's role, communication style, and world
- A memory of every conversation — never summarized away, never reset

---

## How it works

1. At onboarding, the member names their Associate and picks a voice from locally-hosted options
2. The Associate's system prompt is seeded with the member's paradigm (role, communication style, household context)
3. Every voice turn routes through Ollama (local LLM) with the Associate's full persona injected
4. Responses come back in the Associate's chosen voice via local TTS (Kokoro)
5. Every exchange is written to the member's private memory store

---

## Who it's for

Anyone in the household who wants AI that feels personal, not generic. The experience is closest to having a knowledgeable staff member who knows you — except it never sleeps, never forgets, and never shares your information.
