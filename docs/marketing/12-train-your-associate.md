# Train Your Associate

**Tagline:** Teach it who you are. It remembers everything from that moment forward.

---

## What it is

The first time a family member signs into You-Sir Juan™, they walk through a four-step onboarding flow that teaches their Associate Agent the basics: what to call them, what voice to use, and three facts about the household. That's the seed. Every conversation after that adds to it.

---

## Why it matters

An AI that doesn't know you isn't much use. The train-your-associate flow exists to close that gap from day one. Most AI products take months to feel personal — if they ever do. You-Sir Juan™ starts with an explicit invitation: tell us who you are. The Associate listens from the first word.

---

## The four-step flow

### Step 1 — What should I call you?
The member types or speaks their preferred name. Not their full name from the records — the name they actually want their Associate to use. This is stored as a permanent config entry in their memory.

### Step 2 — Pick your Associate's voice
Four voice options, each playing a sample sentence when selected. The member picks the voice that feels right. The choice is stored and applied immediately — every response from that point forward comes in that voice.

### Step 3 — Teach me three things about your household
Three free-form inputs. The member writes anything: routines, names, preferences, facts about the household. ("My daughter's name is Maya." "We have dinner at 7pm." "I prefer summaries, not detail.") Each fact becomes a permanent `training` entry.

### Step 4 — We're set
A celebration screen in the member's paradigm accent color. The Associate gives its first greeting — already using the member's preferred name and voice. The relationship has begun.

---

## Beyond onboarding

Training never stops. The Associate learns from every conversation. Members can also:
- Update preferences through the settings screen at any time
- Speak corrections directly to the Associate ("Actually, call me by my last name")
- Add new household facts conversationally ("By the way, we're moving next month")

Every update is stored immediately and affects all future interactions.

---

## Who it's for

Every family member who signs in for the first time. The flow takes under five minutes. What it produces is an Associate that already knows the member's name, sounds the way they want it to sound, and carries three facts about their life. That's the foundation — and everything after builds on it.
