# Yours to Train

**Tagline:** The AI that belongs to your family — and only your family.

---

## What it is

You-Sir Juan™ is private AI infrastructure that your family trains, owns, and controls. It is not a service you subscribe to. It is not a shared cloud. It runs on your hardware, inside your home, on your network. No one else touches it.

---

## Why it matters

There are voice assistants in millions of homes. You know the ones — you say their name and they respond. They are convenient, widely available, and deeply generic. They do not know your family. They cannot. The companies that built them have no interest in learning your customs, your culture, your language, or the names of the people who matter to you. They are designed for everyone, which means they are fully tailored to no one.

You-Sir Juan™ is built on a different premise.

Your family trains it. Every interaction makes it more yours. It adapts to your culture, not a statistical average of everyone else's. It carries the history of your family's relationship with it — session after session, for as long as you keep it running. Context never resets.

---

## How it works

- The system runs entirely on hardware you own — typically a Mac mini on your home network
- Your family members interact with it via the kiosk interface (iPad Pro) or a web browser
- Every conversation, preference, and lesson you teach it is stored locally and privately
- No telemetry. No training data sent anywhere. Nothing leaves your machines.

---

## Who it's for

Families who want AI that knows them. Family offices that need AI that remembers their operations. Households that value privacy. Anyone who has ever felt like the AI they use doesn't actually know them — and wished it did.

---

## The differentiator in one sentence

Every other AI is trained on the world. This one is trained on your family.
